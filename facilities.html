FACILITIES
  • The classrooms are very well ventilated and well lit. Innovative methods of teaching with the latest techniques will be used to make teaching more meaningful and interesting. Comfortable classroom space for students as per specified norms.
  • A sick room to provide first aid and basic medicare is available
  • The School conducts regular medical check-ups of students by qualified doctors which include check up of eye sight, postural correction, etc.
  • Facilities include games park and indoor activity rooms.
  • A Counselor is always present in the school for the betterment of the students.
  • A well-furnished auditorium with a seating capacity of 800, with acoustics, integrated lighting system ...and the latest projection system provides a superb ambience for variety of school functions.
  • Audio Visual Education to provide varied exposure to the students and to enrich their educational experiences.
  • A well equipped Computer Lab with facility of additional learning.
