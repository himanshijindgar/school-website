AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

Nehru International Public School aims at providing a child centered education with the following aims and objectives.
  1. Providing quality education.
  2. Pursuing excellence in all fields.
  3. Developing team spirit.
  4. Developing the spirit of nationalism and noble Sans Kars.
  5. Inculcating respect for country's cultural heritage.
  6. Enlarging of mental horizons.
  7. Creating a zest for learning
  8. Channelizing youthful energies towards creativity and self-actualization.
  9. Building confidence and positive self-esteem
  10. Develop creativity and imagination.
  11. Inculcating emotional stability along with physical development.
